What in the world are NFTs?

+ -

In short, a Non-fungible token. But what does "Non-fungible" mean? It means it us unique and cannot be replaced by anything else as each NFT has its own unique token. For example, a bitcoin is fungible - trade one bitcoin for another and its the exact same thing. Think of it at pokemon playing cards, but in a digital sense. There are common cards, rare cards and some super rare cards each with their own availavility and uniqueness but instead of them all being the same, NFTs can be anything - from drawings, music all the way to "real estate" in a virtual world!

How do NFTs and the Blockchain work?

+ -

At the highest level, it is safe to say most NFTs are a part of the Ethereum blockchain although there are some other blockchains which implemented their own versions of NFTs which we are looking to eventually add to out site aswell!


But what is a blockchain?

Blockchains are extremely complex which cannot really be explained in a few words accurately. But the basic idea is that blockchains are a way to store data in a decentralized manner, which means there is no one company or entity that controls everything. Instead, its more of a collective agreement between everyone where and how funds and data moves and where it goes.
